## Stable semantics

- pUSD is BUY collateral only; CTF outcome tokens are SELL inventory only.
- Legacy USDC.e balance is tracked separately and must not satisfy pUSD BUY preflight.
- Degraded collateral authority fails closed for BUY and SELL preflight.
- BUY preflight and reservation use actual submitted BUY notional after share quantization: `ceil(shares * limit_price * 1e6)`.
- pUSD balance and pUSD allowance availability are both net of active BUY reservations.
- CTF balance and CTF allowance availability are both net of active SELL reservations.
- CTF inventory and allowance are integer micro-share units; balances floor, required sells ceil.
- Executor entry/exit paths run collateral gates before command persistence or SDK contact.
- Executor ACK paths reserve collateral on the same SQLite connection as the venue command row before SDK contact.
- Terminal venue-command states release collateral reservations through `venue_command_repo.append_event()` in the same savepoint.
- Successful terminal `append_event()` is the only reservation release path after executor ACK/rejection handling; failed terminal appends must leave reservations active.
- `PolymarketClient.get_balance()` is a compatibility wrapper that refreshes and commits a DB-backed CollateralLedger snapshot.
- Direct pUSD redemption is deferred to R1 and fails closed while Q-FX-1 is open.
- Z4 wrap/unwrap APIs record durable command state only; they do not submit live chain transactions.

## Forbidden downstream assumptions

- Do not use pUSD as proof of SELL inventory or CTF tokens as BUY collateral.
- Do not treat allowance as independent of open reservations.
- Do not reserve target notional for BUY orders after share quantization; reserve submitted notional.
- Do not add direct redeem, wrap, or unwrap live side effects before R1/M4/T1 ownership lands.
