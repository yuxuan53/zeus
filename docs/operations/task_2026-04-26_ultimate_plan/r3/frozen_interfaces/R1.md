- `list_commands(conn, *, state=None) -> list[dict]`

## Durable tables

- `settlement_commands`
- `settlement_command_events`

`REDEEM_TX_HASHED` is the recovery anchor. Chain receipt reconciliation moves tx-hashed commands to `REDEEM_CONFIRMED` or `REDEEM_FAILED`.

## Gates and boundaries

- pUSD request/submit is Q-FX-1 gated via `ZEUS_PUSD_FX_CLASSIFIED` + `FXClassification`.
- Unset/mismatched Q-FX-1 fails closed before adapter contact.
- Legacy `USDC_E` payout creates `REDEEM_REVIEW_REQUIRED`, not pUSD accounting.
- Harvester may create redeem intents for winning pUSD claims; it does not directly call adapter redeem.

## Non-goals

- No live redeem side effects in tests/default runtime.
- No production DB mutation.
- No lifecycle grammar changes.
- No automatic position settlement from redeem success/failure; settlement terminalization remains separate.
