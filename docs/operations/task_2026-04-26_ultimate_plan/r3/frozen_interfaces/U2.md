## Stable semantics

- U2 facts are append-only raw provenance, not mutable current-state rows.
- Fresh DBs create `venue_commands.envelope_id NOT NULL`; legacy DBs may have a nullable column from migration, but new writes must pass Python enforcement.
- A new `venue_command` must cite both a fresh executable U1 snapshot and a persisted U2 submission envelope before insertion.
- The envelope gate validates command token, side, price, and size against the persisted envelope; numeric comparisons use decimal-compatible values.
- The envelope gate also validates envelope condition/question/YES token/NO token identity against the executable snapshot when the snapshot row is present.
- Executor entry and exit paths persist a pre-submit `VenueSubmissionEnvelope` before `insert_command()`, V2 preflight, SDK signing, or venue submission.
- Command events remain in `venue_command_events`, but every command event is atomically mirrored into `provenance_envelope_events` with `source`, `observed_at`, `local_sequence`, and `payload_hash`.
- Order-side state grammar lives in `venue_order_facts`; trade-side state grammar lives in `venue_trade_facts`; neither should be collapsed into `CommandState`.
- `venue_trade_facts` is append-only by `(trade_id, local_sequence)` so one trade can record `MATCHED -> MINED -> CONFIRMED` or `MATCHED -> FAILED` without losing raw history.
- Calibration/retraining reads through `load_calibration_trade_facts()` may consume only `CONFIRMED` trade facts; asking for `MATCHED` or `MINED` fails closed.
- `position_lots` separates `OPTIMISTIC_EXPOSURE` from `CONFIRMED_EXPOSURE`; failed matched trades append a `QUARANTINED` lot rather than mutating or promoting optimistic exposure.
- Settlement/redeem trace in U2 is provenance-only via `provenance_envelope_events`; live redemption side effects remain deferred.

## Forbidden downstream assumptions

- Do not add venue order/trade/finality states to `CommandState` because U2 separates raw fact grammars from command intent grammar.
- Do not mutate raw U2 fact rows; derive read models/folds in later phases instead.
- Do not treat `MATCHED` or `MINED` as calibration truth.
- Do not treat `OPTIMISTIC_EXPOSURE` as confirmed exposure or usable confirmed capacity.
- Do not bypass `venue_command_repo` for writes to U2 provenance tables.
- Do not treat U2 provenance closure as live cutover approval; Q1/cutover and downstream M/R/T/G gates still block live deployment.
- Do not implement websocket ingest, exchange reconciliation sweeps, live settlement redemption, risk allocation, or calibration retraining in U2.
