## Initial critic: Dewey the 2nd

Initial verdict: BLOCK

Blockers:
1. `run_reconcile_sweep()` could convert an existing command to `FILLED`/`PARTIAL` from a linkable trade even when the venue trade state was `FAILED` or `RETRYING`.
2. Empty venue enumeration could become orphan/position-drift findings without explicit freshness/success proof.

Remediation:
- `_append_linkable_trade_fact_if_missing()` appends command fill events only for non-FAILED/non-RETRYING trade states.
- `run_reconcile_sweep()` requires adapter read freshness/success proof for open orders, and for trades/positions when those surfaces are available.
- Added regressions:
  - `tests/test_exchange_reconcile.py::test_failed_or_retrying_trade_fact_does_not_advance_command_fill_state`
  - `tests/test_exchange_reconcile.py::test_stale_or_unsuccessful_venue_reads_are_not_absence_proof`
