# 14 — Not Now

Do not do these in the first implementation packets.

## Not now: runtime/source behavior changes

Topology-system reform must not change trading/runtime/source semantics. Source behavior changes require separate packets.

## Not now: graph as authority

Do not make graph output determine authority rank, source validity, settlement truth, or planning-lock waiver.

## Not now: archive default-read

Do not make archives default-read to compensate for thin module books. Extract only selected durable lessons.

## Not now: broad manifest expansion

Do not solve cognition thinness by stuffing more prose-like data into YAML.

## Not now: auto-applying repair drafts

Repair drafts should propose, not mutate. Human/packet review remains required.

## Not now: universal strictness

Do not promote warning-first module book/module_manifest checks to blockers until false-positive rates and repair routes are proven.

## Not now: custom graph refresh machinery

Use official graph status/update/watch/daemon commands before inventing local watchers.

## Not now: deleting topology

Noise is not a reason to delete the routing kernel. It is a reason to scope it.

## Not now: weakening tests to pass

Do not remove laws from tests just to quiet topology_doctor. Promote the law into books/manifest ownership, then adjust tests.

## Not now: giant AGENTS files

Keep AGENTS as routers. Put dense explanation in module books.
